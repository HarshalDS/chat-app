How to Use ChatApp

  1. Sign Up: If you are a new user, click on the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner of the page. Fill in the required details to create a new account.
  2. Login: If you already have an account, click on the "Login" button on the top right corner of the page. Enter your credentials to log in.
  3. Home: Click on the "Home" link in the navigation bar to return to the homepage at any time.
  4. Chat Section: Navigate to the "Chat section" link in the navigation bar to start chatting. Here, you can send and receive messages with other users.
  5. Profile: After logging in, you can view and edit your profile information.
  6. Settings: Use the settings option to customize your chat preferences and notifications.
  7. Logout: Click on the "Logout" button in the navigation bar to log out of your account securely.
  8. Contact Us: If you need further assistance, feel free to contact us through the social media links provided at the bottom of the page or via email.
